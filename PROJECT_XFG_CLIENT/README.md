## üìñ THE PHILOSOPHY
Most servers are static. They wait for a request, process it, and sleep. **Project XFG** is different. It is designed to mimic biological resilience.

It follows the **Cycle of Life:**
1.  **Chaos (The Virus):** Random traffic spikes attack the system. The "organism" takes damage (Fitness drops to 0).
2.  **Adaptation (The Mutation):** The Genetic Algorithm mutates the code in real-time to find a survival strategy.
3.  **Immunity (The Shield):** If the trauma is too high, the system activates a "Lockdown Mode" (Fitness 500). It becomes invincible for 5 seconds.
4.  **Vulnerability (The Withdrawal):** Nature demands balance. After the shield fades, the system is forced into a "Cool-down" phase where it *cannot* protect itself. It must face the chaos again.

It is a simulation of **Resilience**: The ability to break, learn, and rebuild stronger.

## ‚ö° HOW IT WORKS (The Algorithm)

### 1. The Traffic Generator
The system simulates "requests" as biological stressors.
* **Low Stress:** The system mutates normally (Survival Score: 50-200).
* **Critical Stress:** If a "Virus" roll (random < 0.3) hits 3 times in a row, the system enters **CRITICAL FAILURE**.

### 2. The Antibody Response
When consecutive failures occur:
* The **Immunity Shield** triggers.
* **Effect:** Survival Score locks to **500** (Maximum).
* **Duration:** 5 Seconds.

### 3. The Refractory Period
* Once immunity fades, a **10-second timer** starts.
* During this time, the shield **cannot** be reactivated.
* The organism is forced to survive on its own merit.
